Learn at your own pace

Hello, this is Hola. Hola is an online resource for you to start learning Spanish. We know you're always busy. No time to invest on learning a new language. So let us take care of that. But only make sure you persist a regular routine.

Ubiquitous

You can learn anytime, anywhere you feel like it. No pressure. Although you persistency of learning is all it takes.

Challenge yourself

Perhaps the best way to learn is through testing your knowledge. We provide daily challenges that will be accumulated throughout the week. It helps with your long-term memory on the material.

Customized learning

Based on the score you get on your daily challenges, we provide feedback on what you should be more focused on learning. You are welcome to review the material and rechallenge.

Contents of choice

We don't limit the contents you learn. You can choose to learn vocabs, grammars or dialogue... any many more.
